Humans are
innately social.

We believe the best days of social media are ahead of us. The platforms we have today are a first draft, and first drafts get better.

Social media as we know it is ready to be re-shaped and re-defined into something good. Something new. What technologies power that future? What policies protect users? What design decisions lead us there?

We think we can get there sooner. Let's find out.

VanChat

Think of it like a road trip with your friends in a van. Conversations are momentary, intimate, and focused by design.

Private by default

Your data is not for sale. No ad targeting, no data brokers, no surveillance capitalism. Your conversations belong to you.

Safe by design

Built around community-set norms and privacy-preserving nudges that help people repair a conversation before harm spreads.

Canadian-made

Hosted on Canadian soil, designed around Canadian privacy expectations, and shaped by PIPEDA, the Charter, and local accountability.

Canada matters because social spaces need a clear jurisdiction, strong privacy expectations, and product decisions that do not default to surveillance.
