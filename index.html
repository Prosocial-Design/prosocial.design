VanChat

Think of it like a road trip with your friends in a van. Conversations are momentary, intimate, and focused.

Private by default

Your data is not for sale. No ad targeting, no data brokers, no surveillance capitalism. Your conversations belong to you.

Safe by design

Built with trust and safety at its core. Proactive moderation, clear community guidelines, and zero tolerance for hate or harassment.

Canadian-made

A Product of Canada, hosted on Canadian soil and governed by the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms. No data is stored outside of Canada's borders.
